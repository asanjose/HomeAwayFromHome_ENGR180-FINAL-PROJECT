Popular Student Areas

According to a survey which conisisted of about 200 students, these were the most popular areas.

Bellevue Ranch

Pros:

  • Low amount of crimes
  • Close to school
  • College student populated(highest)
  • A decent available of cattracks
Cons:
  • Central area residents are far away from the bus stops
  • No nearby shopping center
  • Higher prices

Average Housemates: 3 (from survey)
El Redondo

Pros:

  • Most available houses
  • Bus stop in the center of the area
  • College student based (2nd highest)
  • Closer to shopping centers
  • Lower Prices
Cons:
  • Tons of crimes in the southern area and its neighboring areas
  • Farther from the school
  • Only 2 cattracks

Average Housemates: 4 (from survey)
Via Moraga

Pros:

  • Close to campus
  • A decent amount of bus stops
  • Little crime
  • Close to food area
  • Lower Prices
Cons:
  • Bus stops are located far away from the central area
  • Fewer listings available
  • Far from general shopping areas

Average Housemates: 5 (from survey)
